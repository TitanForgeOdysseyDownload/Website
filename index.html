FAQ

What version of Minecraft does it use?

TitanForge Odyssey runs on **NeoForge 1.21.1**.

Does it keep a vanilla feel?

Yes — everything is designed to feel like a natural extension of vanilla with more that enough mods.

Are there tech mods?

Only clean, non‑intrusive automation systems that fit the world’s aesthetic.

Is it multiplayer‑friendly?

Absolutely — the pack is optimized for both solo and server play, it has E4MC installed.

Is it Finished

Not yet, it is still in Beta, I am currently creating a finale and the quests/more config.
